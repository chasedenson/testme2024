What makes Meme Vault (MMV) unique and valuable?

REWARDS. Everyone has the power to mint MMVs, through rewards for contributions to the . The reward rate will decrease over time as the contributions increase. The estimated max supply is 23M tokens.

STAKING: MMV hodlers will be able to stake their tokens to earn payouts from fees earned on the platform. In order to avoid dilution of the MMV tokens, payouts will be in ETH, oppose to issuing new MMVs.

NFTs: In addition to MMV rewards, each contributor will be issued an ("Key") representing ownership to the respective meme and vault number. Owners will have the option to sell ownership of their vault.
